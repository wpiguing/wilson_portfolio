About Me

A few things about me...

BIO

Hello there! My name is Wilson, and I'm an aspiring developer. Officially, I'm a web technician for Indiana University Northwest. I help with the ongoing implementation of content in the IUN webspace using the Cascade Server web content management system. Collaborating closely with the campus Office of Marketing and Communications, I also get to assist other content managers in achieving content accessibility.
Which really means . . . I get to make really cool things, and at the same time, help other people with their own cool things!

IU Northwest - Web Technician

As an intern, and eventually as a technician, I was tasked with everyday tasks pertaining to the upkeep of content within the IUN webspace. With the use of the Cascade CMS and knowledge of HTML and CSS, I took part in major collaborative projects such as ADA web content accessibility, framework transitions, and website content refreshes.

IU Northwest - Service Technician

Prior to becoming a part of IUN's Web team, I was a service technician specializing in addressing computer hardware and software problems. I worked closely with users of all types including students, faculty, and staff in troubleshooting issues on both Windows and Mac platforms.

Education - IUN Class of 2017

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in both Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. I was able to make either the Dean's or Chancellor's List in all of my 9 semesters while averaging a 3.78 GPA.

Some Fun Facts

I like spending time with various forms of content creation. There's the obvious such as coding and continuously learning in my spare time. However, I also enjoy covering songs with my piano and guitar. I also love video games, and generating creative videos for both my YouTube and Twitch channels.
