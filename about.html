About Me

A few things about me...

BIO

Hello there! My name is Wilson, and I'm a highly driven, self-motivated developer. Officially, I'm a web technician for Indiana University Northwest where my main task is to help with content management using the Cascade CMS. I also play a key role in IUN's ongoing efforts to achieve ADA accessibility compliance.
But honestly, what I really do is . . . Make really cool and meaningful things that make a difference.

IU Northwest - Web Technician

As an intern, and eventually as a technician, I was tasked with the upkeep of content within the IUN webspace. With the use of the Cascade CMS and knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3, I took part in major collaborative projects such as website content refreshes, ADA web content accessibility, and framework transitions.

Development Experience

My front-end development experience include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and SASS. I've also had formal classroom experience in object-oriented languages such Python and Java. Some Adobe Creative Cloud applications that I have working knowledge with include Illustrator, Photoshop, and Premiere. I strive to constantly improve my skill set, and have been expanding my knowledge of PHP and jQuery. Moreover, I'm also working on the full LAMP stack.

IU Northwest - Service Technician

Prior to becoming a part of IUN's Web team, I was a service technician with experience in addressing computer hardware and software problems. I worked closely with all types of users including students, faculty, and staff in troubleshooting a wide range of issues on both Windows and Apple platforms.

Education - IUN Class of 2017

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in both Computer Information Systems and Business Administration. I was on either the Dean's or Chancellor's List in all of my 9 semesters while averaging a 3.78 GPA. During my time as a student, I was fortunate enough to have been the recipient of IUN's Special Academic Scholarship.

Some Fun Facts

Content creation is a passion of mine. Whether it's coding/learning new technologies or covering songs with my piano and guitar, I spend much of my free time making things that I find enjoyable. I also create videos for both my YouTube and Twitch channels.
